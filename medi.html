Infrastructure is the physical and organizational structure that supports a country, region, or business. It includes the systems and facilities that enable communication, transportation, and the production of goods and services.

Infrastructure projects are plans to improve, maintain, or repair infrastructure. Public sector infrastructure, like roads and bridges, is often funded by taxes. Public works projects, like schools and hospitals, are often a partnership between the public and private sectors.
